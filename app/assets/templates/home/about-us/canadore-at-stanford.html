Canadore College

We are extremely pleased to announce our recent partnership with Canadore College.
 
Canadore College is a public post secondary institution, approved and authorized by the Ontario Ministry of Training, Colleges and Universities and has been in operation and good standing since 1967.
 
The College boasts the highest graduation rate of Ontario’s 24 public colleges, has over 36,000 alumni, and graduates approximately 1,000 students each year.
 
Articulation to Canadore College is available to graduates of specific Stanford International College programs.

For more information, please visit Canadore’s website.
