Why Choose Stanford?

Our goal at Stanford International College is to provide quality, student centered and career focused education which will facilitate the personal growth and employment of our students.Our philosophy is to meet the individual needs of each student and our programs are designed to accommodate current employment requirements. Our success is due to our dedicated and highly trained staff who care about your future success.

 

Our programs are instructor led and our hands-on-approach has proven effective for the learning and retention of knowledge. Our qualified instructors bring years of experience to the classroom and maintain their professional and trade skills.This enables them to share the most current and updated information with their students.Our instructors work closely with students helping them to reach their goals.

 

Choosing  Stanford International College of Business and Technology is the right choice for your future!

 

Here are some of the advantages we offer:

 
Convenient Locations
 
Our campuses are located throughout Southern Ontario and offer free student parking. Four of our campuses are located within the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) with easy access to the exciting and vibrant downtown Toronto area via public transit. Our Kitchener Campus is located in the region of Waterloo which is known as  Canada’s Technology Triangle.
 
Industry Relevant Programs
 
Our programs are designed to meet current industry standards and the practical skills required for employment.
 
Multi-Cultural Staff and Faculty 
 
Our student population consists of people from many countries.  All classes are taught in English, however  in addition to English and French our staff also speak Mandarin, Hindi, Spanish and Korean.
 
Highly Qualified Faculty and Staff
 
Our faculty and staff are qualified, knowledgeable, well-trained, helpful and committed to delivering high quality educational programs. We strive to make your educational journey as rewarding and enjoyable as possible.
 
Flexible Enrollment Options
Our modular delivery schedule offers multiple enrollment dates throughout the year.
 
Small Class Sizes
Personal attention to learning styles will  maximize your learning potential.
 
Strong Partnerships: Articulation Opportunities
 
Graduates wishing to continue their studies will benefit from our educational partnerships.
 
Student Services
 

  • Stanford Employment Services
    Finding employment after graduation is of course a primary goal. Stanford can help. We will assist you with job-search strategies, resume and cover letter writing, interview preparation, mock interviews, employment application forms, current labour market information and any other employment related matter.  We provide support and direction when looking for part-time, full-time or temporary employment.
     

  • Student Support Services
    High priority is always given to student needs at Stanford. We are committed to your academic and personal success and are pleased to offer our students the support they require when working through concerns that may impact their success at the College.  Our dedicated staff is always available to assist you.
